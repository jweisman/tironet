Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 March 2026

Tironet (“we”, “us”, or “our”) operates the Tironet application (https://tironet.org.il), a non-commercial tool designed to assist commanders in managing training processes.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Tironet, we may collect the following information:

Personal Information (via Google OAuth):

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture

Authentication Data:

  • Information necessary to log you into the application (including OAuth and SMS-based authentication)

Technical Data:

  • IP address (for diagnostic and security purposes)
  • Usage and error data collected via Sentry (for debugging and improving reliability)

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Authenticate users
  • Identify users within the application
  • Maintain and improve the functionality and reliability of the service

We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Google User Data

Tironet uses Google OAuth to authenticate users. We only access basic profile information (name, email address, and profile picture) and use it strictly for authentication and identification.

We do not access, store, or use any other Google account data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Data is processed by trusted service providers solely for hosting and infrastructure purposes, including:

  • Vercel (application hosting)
  • Neon (database hosting)
  • Sentry (error monitoring)

These providers process data only as necessary to operate the service.

5. Data Storage and Retention

Your data is stored securely using Neon database services.

We retain your data only as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 6).

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting:

support@tironet.org.il

We will process such requests within a reasonable timeframe.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure authentication via Google OAuth and SMS login

However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Children’s Privacy

Tironet is intended for adults only. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under the age of 18.

9. Jurisdiction

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the State of Israel.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact:

support@tironet.org.il
